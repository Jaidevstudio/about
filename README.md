## Who We Are

Jaidevstudio is an open-source community founded in 2021. We bring together developers from around the world to build projects that make technology more accessible, regardless of language, background, or experience level.

We are not a company. We are a community of people who believe that open source is for everyone.

---

## Our Core Beliefs

### Open Source for Everyone

We are passionate about open source. It is not just for a select few -- it is for everyone. Code should be accessible, transparent, and collaborative. Every contribution, no matter how small, matters.

### Inclusive and Safe Space

Our community is a warm, welcoming space. No matter who you are, where you are from, or what your experience level is, you belong here. We enforce a Code of Conduct to ensure everyone feels safe and supported.

### Welcome New People

New faces bring new ideas. We actively welcome newcomers, mentor first-time contributors, and celebrate every first pull request. If you are new to open source, this is the right place to start.

### Learn by Helping

The best way to grow as a developer is to help others. We encourage members to teach, share knowledge, and lift each other up. When one of us learns, we all learn.

## How to Join

1. Go to our [Support repo](https://github.com/Jaidevstudio/support/issues/new?template=invitation.yml)
2. Fill out the invitation form
3. Our automated system sends you an invite within minutes
4. Accept the invite and set your membership to Public
5. Pick a project and start contributing

---

## How to Contribute

- **Find an issue** -- Look for `good first issue` labels across our repositories
- **Fork and branch** -- Fork the repo, create a branch, make your changes
- **Submit a PR** -- Open a pull request with a clear description
- **Get reviewed** -- A maintainer will review and provide feedback
- **Get merged** -- Your contribution becomes part of the project
